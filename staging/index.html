Who We Are

The Conscious Collective started as a small group of friends, all international residents, psychedelic explorers, living in Madrid.

We wanted to play, and to experiment

...through mindful events, and psychedelic sentiments

...to connect and to build

...a community colourful and fulfilled

...for people just like ourselves, gleeful.

 

If you too, empathize,
come join us, metamorphosize. 🌈

This project (and our community) is an ongoing work in progress - 🙏 please reach out if you would like to help!
